Brick Buy Brick

Our Mission

To allow everyone to access Home Ownership & Property Investment

Getting on to the property ladder in any of the World’s major cities is hard. High deposits and high interest rates - the average first time buyer's deposit in London is now £91k.

Property is known to a be a safe investment and yet due to outdated methods of financing much of society is excluded from investing in it.

We will change this!

Our innovative new way of financing allows everyone to benefit from home ownership far earlier in their lives.

Property can now be owned Brick Buy Brick.

Invest

Now you can invest in property from as little as £50.

Invest

Each property is split into "Bricks" which act like shares. No longer do you need to own the whole thing.

You can now invest in a property from as little as £50.

Earn a Regular Monthly Income

Earn an attractive Fixed Monthly Income on your investment as you would with a Bank.

Exposure to Property Prices

As you own a share of the property - you will see a share in any price movements. Realise these at any time.

Clear Exit Route

List your "Bricks" for sale on the Property Stock Exchange. These can be bought by the Home Owner or any other investor.

Set the price to determine speed of sale.

Looking for a Home

Tired of renting? Brick Buy Brick can help.

Minimal Deposit

No longer do you need to save 8 years for a deposit and spend years renting. Now buy your dream home from as little as 12 months rent.

Interest Rate

Each month pay a fixed % on the "Bricks" you do not own. Due to our crowdfunding model this will beat the Banks.

Flexible Repayment

Each month set a budget for "Brick" repurchasing. Our platform will automatically purchase the cheapest "Bricks" listed for sale.

Selling up?

Simply inform us and your "Bricks" will be re-listed on the platform for sale. We will do the work of finding a new Home Owner.
